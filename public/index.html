Random Pick

Connect your Notion books database to pull a random read from your Want to Read list.

Create one at notion.so/my-integrations → New integration → copy the Internal Integration Token. Then open your books database → ··· menu → Connections → add your integration.
Open your books database as a full page. The URL looks like:
notion.so/Your-Title-DATABASE_ID?v=...
Copy the 32-character ID between the last / and the ?.
Match these exactly as they appear in your database.
